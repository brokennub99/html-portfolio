The Best Movies According to Raeven

My top 3 movies of all time.

Spoiler. They're all anime.

Howl's Moving Castle

Favorite anime movie of all time. Loved the overall storytelling and all the characters.

Ride Your Wave

It's a story about moving on and accepting the past. Perfect for people who needs to move on. Spoile Alert: The movie hurts like hell but it's all worth it.

A Silent Voice

If you have something in your life that you want to overcome, then this movie is perfect for you.
